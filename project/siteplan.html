JMP Guitars

Site Purpose

This serves as an information and retail site for high-quality custom guitars, accessories, and repair services for guitarists of all levels.

Will feature a section for browsing available guitars, a contact for inquiries about customs and services, and artist spotlight.

Scenarios

  • What different types of guitars or customs are available?
  • How can I schedule a guitar repair service?
  • Are these guitars suitable for professional use and performing?
